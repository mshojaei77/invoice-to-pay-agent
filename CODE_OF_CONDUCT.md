# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a productive community:

- being respectful and constructive,
- focusing feedback on the work,
- accepting responsibility for mistakes,
- helping maintain a professional space for finance, automation, and open-source contributors.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or personal attacks,
- publishing private information without permission,
- sustained disruption of discussions,
- knowingly submitting unsafe sample financial data.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer. Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants when needed to keep the project productive and safe.
